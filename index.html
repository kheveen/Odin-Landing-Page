4 Keys Into Your Lifestyle

A balanced diet includes a variety of nutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals in appropriate proportions.

Regular exercise such as walking, jogging, cycling, swimming, or strength training helps improve cardiovascular health, build strength, boost mood, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Sufficient sleep duration and quality play a significant role in cognitive function, mood regulation, immune function, and physical health.

Practices such as mindfulness meditation, deep breathing exercises, yoga, and spending time in nature can help reduce stress levels and promote relaxation.
